Yep, I've crafted my own version of Symbolab – well, sort of. Admittedly, there are some limitations; no fancy graphs and a few of Symbolab's original features are absent. However, I've devised workarounds for these shortcomings, ensuring a comparable experience.
Even the so-called "modern Mod APKs of Symbolab" fall short when it comes to displaying solution steps without a paid subscription. Thankfully, my clone doesn't impose such restrictions – step-by-step solutions are free for all.
But let's be real – this project is just another one of my reverse engineering escapades, a bit of API tinkering here and there. Nevertheless, it's been an enlightening journey, offering insights into the world of Computer Algebra Systems. So, go ahead, enjoy those step-by-step solutions on the house.
